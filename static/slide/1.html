The Problem:

Budgeting between people for a project can be difficult.


Examples

  • Going on a beach trip with roommates
  • Building a website as part of team (Graphics, Cloud costs, etc)
  • School Club Trip Planning

Whether it's a personal, business, or school project, you can be faced with expenses that require a group consensus. The task of agreeing on items to buy can quickly get complicated when you factor in more people.
